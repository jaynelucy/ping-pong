Rules

Ping Pong count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

NB:A negative number has no effect!!

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become ping
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 becomes pong
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 becomes pingpong
